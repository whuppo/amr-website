General Rules The basics that apply to everyobdy

1. Treat everyone with courtesy and respect.

2. Names need to consist of some English letters and be easily pronounced.

On Violation: The player will be asked to fix their name.
On Refusal: If the player refuses to comply, they will be kicked.

3. Be conservative on mic and chat use (This means no singing, spamming, etc.).

1st Offense: The player will be gagged/muted and given a warning.
2nd Offense: The player will be gagged/muted until staff feel the punishment should end.
3rd Offense: The player will be kicked.

4. No ghosting, cheating, hacking, using exploits, or giving yourself an unfair advantage in any way.

Ghosting: The punishments, in order of number of offences, are: warn/mute, kick, 1 day ban.
Hacking: The player will recieve a permanent ban with no chance of being unbanned.

5. Do not troll

6. Do not intentionally change your name to impersonate anyone on the server.

On Violation: The player will be asked to fix their name.
On Refusal: If the player refuses to comply, they will be kicked.

7. No hate speech (Racism, sexism, etc).

1st Offense: The player will be gagged/muted and given a warning.
2nd Offense: The player will be gagged/muted until staff feel the punishment should end.
3rd Offense: The player will be kicked/banned depending on what was said.

8. No Inappropriate/Offensive Usernames or Text Hats.

On Violation: The player will be asked to fix their name.
On Refusal: If the player refuses to comply, they will be kicked.

9. Do not show too much favoritism; a little is OK.

10. Use common sense; members of staff will use their judgment in situations and punish for actions not explicitly covered in the rules or punishments.

11. Blackmailing is unacceptable.

Punishment: The person who broke this rule shall recieve permanent ban with no chance of being unbanned.

12. Do not prop kill team mates; go karts are not covered by this rule, but the pink kill buttons on Military Jail are.

Punishment: The person who broke this rule shall recieve 1 day ban for every teamate killed.

13. No excessively referencing/talking about religion or politics.

14. "No detours and no delays" are implied in all warden orders.

15. Extermination/Execution days, and Crouch Hax days are banned. Feed the Beast day must end if the total guard count totals 2 (including the Warden).

16. Do not propblock teammates or enemies.

1st Offense: The player will be warned.
2nd Offense: The player will be teleported and given a final warning.
3rd Offense: The player will be slain and forced to Prisoner if they are a Guard.
4th Offense: The player will be kicked.

17. Do not talk over the warden, even if you are on a freeday or are rebelling.

Punishment: The player will recieve a gag until staff feel they should be ungagged.

18. Punching vents is a KOSable offence, and ladders to KOS areas are KOS.

19. Participating players (those on the Guard or Prisoner teams) will be kicked after 2 rounds of AFKism, and those in spectator mode will be kicked after 4 rounds of AFKism.

20. No sprays are allowed that contain NSFW content, advertise other servers or communities, or cause the server to lag.

1st Offense: The player will be warned.
2nd Offense: The player will be kicked.
3rd Offense: The player will recieve a 1 day ban.

21. On an Admin Deagle Warday, camping in a way that gives players no fair chance to fight you (I.E. at the exit of a TP or the end of a vent) is considered an exploit.

1st Offense: The player will be warned.
2nd Offense: The player will be teleported to an acceptable area near them.
3rd Offense: The player will be slain.

22. If you find an exploit, report it to a higher up and no one else.

23. Unless you're in a game that requires team damage (I.E. fight day), you may not damage teamates.

Damaging teamates: The player will be warned and slapped/shocked for the damage done.
Killing teamates: The player will be slain.
Note: If it's clear that the perpetrator is trolling, staff may escalate the punishments.

24. PAC hats and trails may be used, provided they are an acceptable size.

On Violation: The player breaking this rule will be asked to comply to our standards.
On Refusal: If the player refused the request, the player will receive a Kick.
2nd offense The player will be banned, with staff deciding on the length on a case by case basis.

Warden Rules These only apply to the warden

1. Wardens MUST have a mic in order to give orders. Wardens cannot give orders via text chat, but they can designate another guard who has a mic to be warden, provided they agree.


Note: If a player repeatedly opts into Warden but has no mic, he/she will be team swapped.

2. All of the warden's orders must be clear (so that all prisoners may hear and understand them).

3. The warden must repeat an order/koses once when asked to do so by a prisoner; once a new order/kos is added, the counter resets. Common sense may be used, for example if prisoners repeatedly ask for repeats after every order, the warden may opt to not repeat.

On Violation: The will be made aware of this rule and told to repeat his/her orders and KOS's.
On Refusal: If the refuses to repeat, every Prisoner killed for not following their orders will be counted as a victim of RDM.

4. Wardens cannot do two special days in a row (freeday, warday, hide and seek day, etc.). If the player count is 5 or less, this rule does not apply.

1st offense The Warden will be warned.
2nd offense The Warden will be slain.
3rd offense The Warden will be slain and team swapped

5. Wardens MUST explain the game modes if the prisoners do not understand.

On Violation: The will be made aware of this rule and told to explain the game.
On Refusal: If the refuses to explain the game, every Prisoner killed for not either not participating or as a result of the game will be counted as a victim of RDM.

6. Wardens cannot give orders that result in a KOS regardless of what the Prisoners do.

Punishment Every kill as a result of a double KOS situation will count as RDM.

7. The last prisoner must be given LR by the warden. This does not apply if the last prisoner is KOS or if the prisoner is on freeday. The Last Request cannot break a rule and can only apply to one round. The warden can allow prisoners currently on freedays to compete if they request to.

Invalid denial of LRThe Warden will be slain.

8. If no valid order is given by 9:00, the cell doors are not opened before 8:30, or the cell doors are opened before a valid order is given, it is a freeday.

9. On Hide and Seek days, the last prisoner MUST be given LR unless they have rebelled, or the warden has died.

10. Do not use wordplay to KOS prisoners. This includes subtle word changes, additions, or absences. IE Do a 360, last reaction couch, and many other forms of wordplay.

1st offence The Warden will be warned to stop.
2nd offence The Warden will be slain.

11. Constantly killing prisoners using only tricks will be considered trolling.

12. Do not abuse server specific knowledge to KOS prisoners. This includes running games without stating them as starting, leading prisoners into KOS areas, and tricks that are not generally based in logic but in an understanding of our server. While you must still follow server rules, you may not KOS prisoners using these tricks.

13. KOS's =/= orders. If you make something KOS and open cell doors with no orders, it is a freeday. If you revoke all KOS's, prisoners would still be rebelling if the ran, or entered a KOS zone.

14. When an order is given to go between the warden waypoints, being inside the waypoints themselves counts as being in between.

15. The warden may deny LR's that are blatantly unreasonable, or meant to troll (I.E. freeday for everyone but two people, or a day where the warden must obey the prisoners.

16. The Warden may not give a Prisoner Warden powers.

17. Turning team damage on randomly (not for a day that requires team damage) is not allowed.

18. The Warden may apply basic slayable offences to Guards (mic, leaving a certain area, etc) if a special day (I.E. hide and seek day) calls for it, if the guards have repeatedly abused the voice command, or in other cases where the warden deems them necessary to smoothly run the day. Slayable offences pertaining to the use of certain weapons may be applied to Prisoners during special days when the use of said weapons would severely detract from enjoyment of the day (I.E. Stunstick wardays and knife wardays). Slayable offences may not be used to troll or target a specific person unfairly.

Guard Rules These apply to all Guards, the Warden included

1. Do not RDM prisoners.

1 kill: The player will be slain.
2 kills: The player will be slain and team swapped.
3-5 kills: The player will recieve a 5 day ban.
Note: For a full list of punsihments, check out our "Offences and Punishments" page on the forums

2. Follow all warden orders. Not doing so may result in a slay or a team-switch.

3. Guards give basic orders to Prisoners to ensure their duties can be carried out efficiently (I.E. telling a Prisoner to move back from their cell doorway in order to kill a rebel even though the Prisoners were told to AFK freeze).

4. If the Warden dies it's a freeday unless the day is currently a Hide and Seek day or a Warday.

5. Guards can shoot prisoners if they're holding a gun, even if they drop it.

6. Last Guard kills all (LGKA) is activated when out of a core group of three Guards, two are killed normally. For example: on a team of four Guards if one is slain but two are killed by Prisoners, LGKA applies. However, if two were slain and one killed, it would not apply. It does not matter how many Guards there are at the beginning of the round; as long as two were killed by Prisoners, it would apply. If LGKA is in effect, the last guard must actively seek out prisoners to kill them.

On Violation: The Guard will be ordered to commence LGKA
On Refusal: If the Guard refuses to comply, they will be slain.

7. Guards may NEVER aid and abet the prisoners.

8. Do not camp in KOS Areas for more than one minute. This includes just standing outside of a KOS area in the doorway, or the path to the KOS area close-by.

1st offence: The player will be warned.
2nd offence: The player will be teleoprted and given a final warning.
3rd offence: The player will be slain.

9. Do not gunplant.

1st offence: The player will be warned.
2nd offence: The player will be slain and given a final warning.
3rd offence: The player will be kicked.

10. Guards cannot force a prisoner to drop a weapon.

11. Guards may not use cell door controls unless the warden instructs them to, the warden is AFK (If they are afk at the start of the round, wait until 8:59 to open the doors, it's possible for them to come back and give an order in time), or round time is within 5 seconds of 8:30.

1st offence: The Guard will be warned.
2nd offence: The Guard will be slain.
3rd offence: The Guard will be slain and team swapped.

12. Do not guard-block. This is where you purposefully stand in a prisoner's way to prevent them from going somewhere (ie. A guard blocking a prisoner from going through a door/vent).

1st offence: The Guard will be warned.
2nd offence: The Guard will be slain.
3rd offence: The Guard will be slain and team swapped.

13. Freeshooting is not allowed. Hitting someone in the face with a smoke grenade is considered freeshooting as it causes damage.

1st offence: The Guard will be slapped/shocked for the damage done and warned.
2nd offence: The Guard will be slain.
3rd offence: The Guard will be slain and team swapped.

14. Guards may not close the cell doors under ANY circumstances.

15. Only the warden may grant lrs and kill the prisoner after the granting of the lr, unless they give permission to the guards.

16. If a guard gets damaged and is alone with a prisoner, or there is no logical way that another prisoner could have damaged them, he/she may kill the prisoner on the assumption that they damaged them, even if they did not witness it.

17. Commiting suicide is considered trolling.

1st offence: The Guard will be warned.
2nd offence: The Guard will be team swapped.
3rd offence: Staff may use their judgment.

18. If no prisoners are competing for LR, all prisoners will be KOS at 2:00.

Prisoner Rules No, killing Guards isn't RDM

1. If you're RDMed, report it by using Admin chat (@message). Please include as many details as possible, be respectful, and patient.

2. Prisoner-blocking is not allowed. This is where you purposefully stand in a guard's way to prevent them from going somewhere (ie. A prisoner blocking a guard from going through a door or leaving a cell).

1st offence: The Prisoner will be warned.
2nd offence: The Prisoner will be slain.
3rd offence: Staff may use their judgment.

16. If a guard gets damaged and is alone with a prisoner, or there is no logical way that another prisoner could have damaged them, he/she may kill the prisoner on the assumption that they damaged them, even if they did not witness it.

3. Constantly trolling the warden by talking over them, or by spamming "freeday?" or anything similar is considered trolling.

1st offence: The Prisoner will be gagged/muted until staff feel they have learned their lesson.
2nd offence: The Prisoner will be slain.
3rd offence: Staff may use their judgment.

Staff Rules Optional for Higher-Ups

1. Do not abuse your powers.

Punishment: Higher-ups decide on a case by case basis

2. If you're inactive for 2 weeks without a notice of inactivity, you will be demoted. If a member of staff has a track record of poor activity (I.E. repeatedly offline for days on end without a notice), then higher ups reserve the right to use their judgment and demote them before the official two week mark if deemed fit.

3. You must have a valid reason for banning/kicking someone.

4. If you wrongly promote/demote others, you will be demoted permanently, and their ranks will be reverted.

5. Do not speak negatively of the server or the staff in the jailbreak game. You are entitled to your opinions, and you can share them on the forums.
